What is The Crocheted Crumb?

Slow, textured pieces for people who love warm kitchens and long walks.

The Crocheted Crumb blends handmade crochet totes with small-batch cookies. Every piece is stitched slowly and baked in tiny batches, meant to feel like a recipe card passed down and a bag you’ve carried for years.

Handmade Crochet Totes

Thick, textured yarn, neutral tones, and city-friendly sizing. Designed for school runs, coffee dates, and train rides with a book.

Brown Butter Cookies

Soft centers, crisp edges, and sea-salt tops. Think cozy apartment kitchen, even when you’re on the go.

Thoughtful Packaging

Kraft paper, twine bows, handwritten batch labels — everything feels like a tiny gift, even if it’s for you.
