About

The Crocheted Crumb is a tiny, cozy corner of yarn and warm cookies.

A micro-brand built around slow comfort, textured pieces, and the feeling of rainy city mornings.

The idea

The Crocheted Crumb started as a small ritual: a crochet project on the couch, cookies in the oven, and a favorite playlist in the background. Totes and treats slowly turned into a way to share that feeling with other people — a mix of handmade crochet and small-batch baking with a warm, city-cozy mood.

Every piece is intentionally simple, textured, and neutral so it can slip into your everyday life: train commutes, school days, study sessions in a café, or weekend walks with friends.

The details

  • Neutral, oatmeal-cream palettes with espresso and warm brown butter tones.
  • Crochet totes stitched slowly — no mass production, no rush, just one piece at a time.
  • Brown butter chocolate chip cookies with soft centers and sea salt tops.
  • Kraft paper packaging, twine bows, and handwritten batch labels for each order.
